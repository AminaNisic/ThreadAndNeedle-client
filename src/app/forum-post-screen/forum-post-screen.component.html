Hi Sarah!
If you do not have much experience with sewing, I would recommend starting with simple A-line dress! As for fabrics, I would recommend cotton since it is simple to work with as well as pretty breathable. Alternative to that could be a light linen material, since it is not hard to work with. When it comes to hemming, I think that blind or rolled hem might be the easiest to start with. You have plenty of good tutorials on this website for that, so you should look at those. I wish you all the luck, and remember to not get too frustrated if you mess up!
